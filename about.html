32,000 Sq Ft Manufacturing Facility

Committed to a
Sustainable Future

Since 2008, An Noor Packaging & An Noor Trims has been at the forefront of sustainable packaging innovation. Equipped with state-of-the-art machinery from Taiwan, Germany, China, and India, we combine environmental responsibility with exceptional quality standards.

Our dedicated team of 150+ skilled professionals brings extensive industry knowledge and technical expertise to every project. We understand the evolving needs of the global apparel, textile, and retail industries — delivering flexible services, premium products, competitive lead times, and market-responsive solutions.

Operating from our 32,000 sq ft manufacturing facility in Bangladesh with a production capacity of 2,500 M.T. per year, we serve leading brands across USA, Canada, and Europe. As a proud member of BGAPMEA, we are committed to building lasting partnerships founded on trust, innovation, and unwavering quality.

Our Commitment to Environmental Sustainability

Our Vision

To be recognized worldwide as the foremost provider of sustainable packaging solutions, setting industry standards for innovation, quality, and environmental stewardship.

We envision a future where every package tells a story of responsibility — protecting products while preserving our planet for generations to come.

Global Packaging Excellence
MISSION

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive, eco-friendly packaging solutions that exceed global quality standards while minimizing environmental impact.

We achieve this through customer-centric partnerships, continuous innovation, operational excellence, and an unwavering commitment to sustainability. Our mission is to be the trusted packaging partner that helps brands showcase their products responsibly and beautifully.

An Noor Packaging Industries is a leading Bangladesh-based manufacturer and global exporter of premium packaging solutions — serving fashion and retail brands across 20+ countries since 2008.
