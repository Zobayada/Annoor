About An Noor

We Deliver Packaging Solutions Trusted Worldwide

Founded in 2008 in Bangladesh, An Noor Packaging & An Noor Trims has grown into a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality packaging products and garments accessories. We specialise in custom packaging boxes, labels & tags, poly bags, adhesive solutions, and eco-friendly packaging — serving global brands with export-grade quality.

Our state-of-the-art 32,000 sq. ft. facility combines advanced machinery from Taiwan, Germany, China, and India with skilled craftsmanship to deliver precision products. We source premium raw materials from Taiwan, Indonesia, Thailand, Korea, Malaysia, China, India, USA, and Australia — all meeting OEKO-TEX standards and international compliance certifications including ISO, BSCI, and FSC.
